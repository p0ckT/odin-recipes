Peanut Butter and Jelly

~Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves jelly spread on bread.

~Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly or Jam

~Steps

  1. Take one slice of bread and coat well in peanut butter
  2. Take anothers slice of bread and coat with jelly
  3. Mush both pieces of bread together
  4. Eat!

Hot Dogs

~Description

A hot dog is a food consisting of a grilled or steamed sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun.

~Ingredients

  • Bread Bun
  • Hot Dog
  • Tomato and Mustard Sauce
  • Relish

~Steps

  1. Pour water into a pot and take it to boil on a stove
  2. Gently place your hot dogs in the water as not to burn your hand from spiting water
  3. Boil hot dogs for 5 to 7 minutes or when they are cooked through
  4. Remove hot dogs from water with tongues and place in bread bun
  5. Add desired toppings
  6. Eat!

Chicken Parma

~Description

Chicken parmigiana, or chicken parmesan (Italian: pollo alla parmigiana), is a dish that consists of breaded chicken breast covered in tomato sauce and mozzarella, parmesan, or provolone cheese.

A quantity of ham or bacon is sometimes added. The dish originated from 20th-century Italian diaspora.

~Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Ham or Bacon
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

~Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in shallow baking dish and spoon over tomato sauce
  2. Place ham over tomato sauce
  3. Cover with mozzarella cheese evenly
  4. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through
  5. Eat!
